Monthly data entry

Enter raw monthly counts. Revenue in ₹ Lakhs. Cells turn yellow while editing — click Save year to persist. LSCS rate is calculated automatically.

IVF / IUI / Follicular

Enter total cycles and total conceived per period. Success rate auto-calculates. If both monthly and annual are entered for the same year, monthly takes priority and annual is treated as legacy fallback.
Years with monthly data show their auto-summed totals (read-only). Years without monthly data are editable for legacy entry.

Annual totals (legacy data)

For older years where only annual totals are available. Each value is auto-divided by 12 and shown as estimated in monthly views. Revenue in ₹ Lakhs. If you later enter monthly data for the same year, monthly entries take priority and overwrite the estimate.

Expected deliveries — next months

Formula: Predicted deliveries for month M = (mean of last year's same-month deliveries + USG done 4 months back × 1.1) ÷ 2.
Example: Prediction for May uses USG count from January × 1.1, averaged with last May's actual delivery count.

Prediction accuracy (history)

Compares previous predictions against actuals once both are available.

Custom service categories

Add new service categories beyond the built-in ones. They appear automatically as columns in Monthly Entry and as chips on the Dashboard.

Google Sheets sync

Paste the Web App URL from your Apps Script deployment (ends in /exec). Once set, Sync (⟳) pulls the latest from the Sheet and Save (☁) pushes local changes back. If left blank, the dashboard works offline using browser storage only.

About this dashboard

Single-user hospital performance tracker. Data persists locally via window.storage. Calendar year basis (Jan–Dec). All counts are integers; revenue in ₹ Lakhs (auto-displayed as Crores when ≥ 100 L).
LSCS rate = LSCS ÷ (Normal + LSCS) × 100. IVF/IUI/Follicular success rate = Conceived ÷ Total cycles × 100. TTM = sum of trailing 12 months.
